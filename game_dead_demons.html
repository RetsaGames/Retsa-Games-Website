Dead Demons

Kill demons, unlock weapons & paint the levels with blood.

Welcome to Dead Demons, an ultraviolent game where you have to survive infinite waves of perverted demons, using all kinds of medieval weaponry.

Use arrows-keys to move and jump, Z to attack.
