GREAT SITES

This is a list of great sites i found. No ads, no bs:


Cibercirujas

(in spanish) Argentinian community of hacktivists fighting agains planned obsolecense. They recover old hardware, make crazy inventions with electronics & host amazing gatherings.


viznut

This is the site of Viznut (a.k.a. Ville-Matias Heikkilä), a coder from the demoscene who is also known for coining the term "permacomputing" —the application of permaculture principles to technology. Viznut's writings are essential reading for advocates of degrowth, which offers an alternative to our current death-cult of productivity.


Webring

Just a list of cool sites: journals, hand-crafted wikis & portfolios.


Unreal Archive

I always come back to playing retro FPS games, specially the ones that have ton of content created by the community. Two of my favourites are the original Unreal & unreal torunament. This site is an archive of maps and mods from all the games in the Unreal series.


Veronica Explains

Tutorials and blogs about Linux, software and retro-tech


wassimulator

Devlogs, programming blogs & miscellaneous stuff. Has a amazing deep-dive on racing game physics.
