Homunculus Surgeon

Your humanoid creations are all messed up.

Use a scalpel to separate as many siamese twin homunculus as you can in the given time.

Be careful and don't harm your creations!

Controls:

Mouse - Move the scalpel.

Mouse left button - hold and release to make a cut.
