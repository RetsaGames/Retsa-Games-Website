Metal Vomit

Metal Vomit is a Mecha themed shoot-em-up played with just 1 button.

You are Vomit, a Mech pilot fighting an evil regime that uses skull shaped drones to keep its citizens in line.

Made for the Blackthornprod Game Jam #3 with the theme "less is more". The game implements the theme by it's minimalist design: the game controls only consist of pressing and holding the spacebar, the only colors are on the greyscale and everything is made of a very limited set of graphical assets.
