Bubblemancerz

Puzzle fighter where you cast bubbles to defeat your opponent.

Join 4 or more bubbles of the same color to score, score multiple times in a short amount of time to make combos.

Fight other players in the local multiplayer or defeat the bots in single player.

Also contains an "spectator" mode where you watch 2 bots fight against each other.

Keyboard Controls:

First player

  • Movement: A,D
  • Shoot: W
  • Switch bubbles: S

Second player

  • Movement: Left/Right
  • Shoot: Up
  • Switch bubbles: Down

Xbox controller controls:

  • Movement: Left stick
  • Shoot: A
  • Switch bubbles: B
